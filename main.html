elden ring

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment it takes place in the realm of the Lands Between, sometime after the destruction of the titular Elden Ring and the scattering of its shards, the Great Runes.

    When you first fire up a new game, you're presented with ten classes—or "origins"—to choose from, each gives your character different base stats and starting level. This choice also determines the weapons with which you'll start the game.

    the classes are

  1. Vagabond
  2. Warrior
  3. Hero
  4. Bandit
  5. Samurai
  6. Prophet
  7. Prisoner
  8. Wretch
